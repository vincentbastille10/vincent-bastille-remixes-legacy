FEEL EVERY

You already know these songs. You've lived inside them. That's exactly why this album exists.

Vincent Bastille doesn't remix. He excavates — pulling the soul out of a classic and rebuilding it with surgical restraint and club instinct.

Each track moves between two worlds: the dancefloor that never lies, and the quiet that follows 4am. House without nostalgia. Emotion without sentimentality.

This is what happens when a lifetime of listening meets a career of knowing what moves people.

The Artist

A LIFE
IN MUSIC

Vincent Bastille is not a producer chasing trends. He is a student of the dancefloor — one who has spent decades learning what moves people and why.

Based in Le Mans, his sound travels: house, disco, ambient, club. Elegant but never cold. Precise but never sterile. He carries the underground and the international in equal measure.

With over 56 releases in his catalog, Remixes (Legacy) is his most focused work — a distillation of everything he knows about transforming music into experience.
