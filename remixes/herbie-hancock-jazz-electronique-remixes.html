The Pioneers of Jazz-Electronics: Herbie Hancock and Beyond

Herbie Hancock's influence on jazz-electronic music cannot be overstated. A pioneer of the genre, Hancock's 1970s album "Head Hunters" is often cited as one of the first true jazz-funk albums. And yet, Bastille's remixes of tracks like "Chameleon" and "Keep Your Head Up" show that there's still much to be gleaned from Hancock's legacy. As he puts it, “I wanted to take these classic tracks and give them a new lease on life, to show that even the most timeless music can be modernized and reimagined.”

The Legacy of Jazz-Electronics in Modern Music
