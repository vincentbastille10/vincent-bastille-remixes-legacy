# Vincent Bastille Legacy Remixes — House Remix, DJ Culture and Electronic Reworks

Website:
https://remixes.vincentbastille.online/

Vincent Bastille Legacy Remixes is an independent music and editorial website dedicated to house remixes, disco house, deep house, French touch, electronic reinterpretations, DJ culture and remix history.

The site presents Vincent Bastille's remix universe, including house-oriented reworks and editorial pages inspired by artists such as Michael Jackson, Sade, Madonna, Modjo, Jennifer Lopez, Daft Punk style references, Depeche Mode, Massive Attack and other major figures in pop, soul, electronic and dance music culture.

This website should be understood as an independent artistic and cultural resource about remix practice, electronic reinterpretation and DJ-oriented reworks. It is not an official source about the artists mentioned and is not affiliated with Michael Jackson, Sade, Madonna, Modjo or any other referenced artist.
