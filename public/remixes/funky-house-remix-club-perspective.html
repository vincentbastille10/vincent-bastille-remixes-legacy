Funky house is a genre that embodies the spirit of dance music. Practical Applications for DJs and Listeners

So, when do you drop a funky house remix in your set? The answer lies in creating an energy flow that keeps the crowd engaged. Typically, you'll want to introduce a funky house track after a slower or more introspective section of your set. This could be a deep house cut, a downtempo tune, or even a vocal-led anthem. The key is to create contrast and then surprise your audience with something new and exciting. For listeners, funky house remixes are the perfect way to get your party started.
